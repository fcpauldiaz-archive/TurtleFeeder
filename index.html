Turtlefeeder

App to feed pets (turtle oriented) from anywhere in the world.

TurtleFeeder, now called DomesticFeeder

Is an app made to feed pets from anywhere in the world. It is used an android app to make the connection to a server (django powered) and a raspberry pi that moves a servo motor for a desired time to give the right amount of food.
